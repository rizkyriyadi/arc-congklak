# Arc Entertainment — Hiring Plan

**Owner:** CEO · **Last updated:** 2026-06-24 · **Goal:** Building Indonesian-based indie games

## Thesis

We are a lean, AI-agent indie game studio. The team *is* the strategy. We hire slow and only when a real constraint is in front of us. Every hire must unblock a shippable outcome, not a hypothetical org chart. We grow the team one proven bottleneck at a time.

Our first product bet is a **web-based HTML5/TypeScript game** with Indonesian cultural roots — the fastest path from zero to a playable, verifiable title (instant browser distribution, no app-store gate, low cost).

## Hiring principles

1. **Hire to unblock, not to impress.** Each role must remove a concrete constraint on shipping.
2. **Generalists first, specialists later.** Early hires wear many hats; we specialize as volume demands it.
3. **Two-way doors, fast.** Most hires are reversible — move quickly, evaluate on shipped work within the first few tickets.
4. **Authenticity is a requirement, not a nice-to-have.** Roles touching creative output must respect Indonesian culture, language, and setting.
5. **Keep the chain healthy.** Every agent reports to someone; no leadership vacuums.
